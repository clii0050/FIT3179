　　　　WORLD MAP (HP score)



This is a World map showing the happiness score of each country in the world. However, because of some countries or regions that the dataset didn't have, the map will display Grey to represent that there is no data for that country.



There are 6 regions, or continents, which are Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America and Oceania. The color of a country represents the happiness score. The higher the score the country is, the color will be close to green. while lower scores will display as red.





This is a box plot showing the Median, max, min etc... However, Oceania didn't show on the plot, because there are only 2 countries belonging to Oceania is the data set, so it will not display.

Relationship between GDP & Happniess Score

(click legend to highlight)



Higher GDP, higher score?



Overall, Yes. As the graph shown, the higher Gross domestic product of a country, the country would likely has higher happiness score .



If you scroll the min GDP bar to 1.1, you will soon find out that 90% of those countries have happiness score with more than 5.



As a result, people who live in countries that have higher Gross domestic product will be happier, because those countries will likely have higher happiness scores.
